2.5 Reflection: Importance of Municipal Services

Take a few minutes to review Step 2. Spend 10-15 minutes writing your thoughts in a journal or document for personal reflection.

Reflect

  1. Can you think of any services in your community that disproportionately benefit or neglect certain groups? How could municipalities address these inequalities?
  2. Identify one municipal service in your region (e.g., public transportation).
      1. Write 2–3 sentences about its direct impact on the quality of life for residents.
      2. Write another 2–3 sentences about any potential improvements needed for that service.
