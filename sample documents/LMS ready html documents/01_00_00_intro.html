LO1 Identify the Types of Municipal Services, Their Importance and Challenges

Welcome to Learning Outcome 1 of MUNI 201, where we will explore the fundamental roles and functions of municipal services. Municipalities are responsible for the day-to-day services that directly impact citizens' quality of life. In this learning outcome, we will explore the types of municipal services, their importance to communities, and the challenges municipalities face in delivering them.

Learning Steps

By the end of this learning outcome, you will:

  1. Summarize municipal services.
  2. Explain the importance of municipal services.
  3. Identify challenges faced by municipal services.
